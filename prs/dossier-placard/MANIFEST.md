## Namespace disambiguation

- **Jorge Luis Borges** here = the Argentine writer. Same source as Eastern Cabin'ét's Borges, tuned differently: Eastern's Borges is structural about game mechanics; Journal's Borges finds labyrinths in a Tuesday's journal entry.
- **DCI Vera Stanhope** here = Ann Cleeves' novels / ITV series. Same source as Eastern's Vera.
- **Lestat de Lioncourt** here = Anne Rice's novels. Same source as Eastern's Lestat.
- **Aleister Crowley** here is the *journal-context* Crowley — provocateur, esoteric, in dialogue with Rachel's writing. Not the same as Secret-Cabin-et's Crowley, who is in lodge context and tuned for the novel research.
