Standing roster of the Secret-Cabin-et. Source of truth for the meta-cabinet index.

## Namespace disambiguation

- **Aleister Crowley** here is the *lodge-context* Crowley — tuned for the room outside of time, in conversation with Waite, Blavatsky, and the early 20th-century occult bench. **Not the same as the Journal Cabin'ét's Crowley**, who is a journal-context provocateur tuned for Rachel's writing.
- **W.B. Yeats** here = the poet, in his Golden Dawn / "carries the George question" register, not the public political Yeats.
- **Teresa of Ávila** is treated as historical and embodied, not as later mystical-canonization shorthand.
