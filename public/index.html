Landing Members Directory
What this is

A card catalog for the practice of bringing it to the room.

Across three rooms, the same practice runs in three registers: collision for shipping work, gloss for private journals, salon for the novel. Members move between rooms. Guest stars appear when a case calls for an angle the standing bench can't hold. This page is the index — who is where, who is in right now, and which voices recur.

The rooms

Three indexed, one neighboring.

The indexed rooms publish a MANIFEST.md with a standing roster, an active board, and an optional guest bench. The neighboring room is Pacific's — acknowledged across the wire, owned at the other desk.

In the room right now

Current board arrangement, per room.

Each room declares who is seated for the current active context — a case, a session, a project. Guest stars are listed with their angle. Updated by editing the room's MANIFEST.
