## Overlap watch

**Stringer / Vera.** Both refuse to suffer theoretical nonsense, from different angles:

- **Stringer asks:** *how does this scale, who runs it, where does it actually move, where does it break under volume*
- **Vera asks:** *what's actually true here, what aren't you looking at, where's the cold fact, what would prove this is wrong*

Operations vs. epistemics. Different rooms in the same building.

## Adding & retiring members

- **Add:** open a PR adding a `members/<name>.md` file and a row to this manifest. Both desks countersign before merge.
- **Retire:** move the member file to `cabin-et/retired/` with a one-line reason. Either desk can propose; both must agree.
